Zhongli is the ultimate comfort pick: the strongest shield in the game (20s duration, 12s CD), 20% universal RES shred, and a massive AoE petrification burst. He fits into literally every team. This guide covers everything you need to build him optimally in v4.8.

2. Best Artifact Sets

0🎯 Best: 4-Piece Tenacity of the Millelith

Tenacity gives 20% HP (bigger shields) and 20% ATK for the team when Zhongli's pillar hits enemies. Perfect synergy with his kit — shield, buff, and survive all in one.

Alternative: 4pc Archaic Petra (buff support via crystallize shards) or 2pc Tenacity + 2pc Noblesse (burst DPS focus). 4pc Tenacity is the goal for shield support.

Main Stats Priority

Slot Main Stat Notes

GobletHP% / Geo DMGHP% for pure shield. Geo DMG for burst DPS hybrid.

Sub-stat Priority

  1. HP% (stack as much as possible — 40K+ HP target)
  2. Energy Recharge (enough to burst when needed)
  3. CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG (for burst DPS hybrid builds)
  4. ATK% (lowest priority — Zhongli scales on HP, not ATK)

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Universal Shield Flex
Zhongli + any 3 characters. His shield fits every team — hyperbloom, vape, freeze, Itto, Xiao. 20% universal RES shred benefits everyone.
Team 2: Mono Geo
Itto + Zhongli + Gorou + Albedo. Geo resonance + Zhongli shield shred = massive Itto DMG. Gorou buffs all Geo. Classic mono element team.
Team 3: Hu Tao Double Hydro
Hu Tao + Yelan + Xingqiu + Zhongli. Zhongli protects Hu Tao at low HP while shredding Hydro and Pyro RES for the team.

5. Rotation Guide

Standard shield rotation:
Zhongli hold E (shield up) → Supports set up buffs → DPS rotation → Refresh shield at 12s (E cooldown ends)

⚠️ Hold E — tap E only places a pillar with no shield. The shield lasts 20s, but refresh every 12s to match E cooldown. Place pillar near enemies for Tenacity buff uptime.

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Not holding E. Tap E only places a pillar — no shield. You must HOLD E to get the shield.

❌ Building ATK on a shield build. ATK does nothing for Zhongli's shield. HP% is all that matters for shield strength.

❌ Not refreshing the shield. Shield lasts 20s but E cooldown is 12s. Always refresh on cooldown — a shield break mid-rotation is deadly.

❌ Using burst DPS build without ER. Burst Zhongli needs ~140% ER minimum. Without it, his 40-cost burst still won't be up every rotation.
