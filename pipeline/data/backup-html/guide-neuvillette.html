Neuvillette Build Guide

💧 Hydro · 📖 Catalyst · S Tier · Main DPS

The Ordainer of Inexorable Judgment. Devastates enemies with a continuous Hydro beam that scales with HP. Self-sustaining and monstrously powerful.

Neuvillette is one of the strongest on-field Hydro DPS characters in Genshin Impact. His charged attack beam deals continuous AoE damage that scales entirely with HP — not ATK. With 4pc Marechaussee Hunter providing 36% free CRIT Rate, you can stack CRIT DMG to absurd levels. This guide covers everything you need to build him optimally in v4.8.

2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4-Piece Marechaussee Hunter

Neuvillette's charged attack constantly drains and heals his own HP, triggering Marechaussee's 36% CRIT Rate bonus effortlessly. This frees you to stack CRIT DMG to 250%+ while maintaining a 1:2 ratio. With his HP-scaling, this set is essentially custom-made for him.

Alternative: 4pc Heart of Depth (if you haven't farmed Marechaussee yet). 2pc HP% + 2pc Hydro DMG (transitional build).

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Neuvillette Hypercarry
Neuvillette · Furina · Kazuha · Baizhu. His premium team. Furina provides massive DMG% and enables his A1 passive. Kazuha VV shred + grouping. Baizhu provides shields and healing for Furina stacks. Highest ceiling.
Team 2: Budget Freeze/Hypercarry
Neuvillette · Fischl · Sucrose · Zhongli. Zhongli shield is essential at C0 for interruption resistance. Fischl provides consistent electro application and off-field damage. Sucrose provides VV shred and EM/grouping.
Team 3: Taser (Electro-Charged)
Neuvillette · Fischl · Beidou · Kazuha/Sucrose. Double Electro for particle generation. Beidou's burst chains through Neuvillette's charged attack beam. High AoE damage and stagger resistance.
Team 4: Hyperbloom Driver
Neuvillette · Nahida · Raiden (Full EM) · Flex (Zhongli/Baizhu). Neuvillette drives hyperblooms with sustained Hydro application. Raiden triggers from off-field. Extremely high floor damage with minimal investment.

Normal Attack talent governs his charged attack beam damage — crown it first. Skill and Burst are roughly equal priority after NA. Level both to 9 before crowning either.

⚠️ C0 vs C1: At C0, Neuvillette's charged attack can be interrupted — you need a shielder (Zhongli, Baizhu, Layla). C1 provides full interruption resistance, freeing a team slot. If you main Neuvillette, C1 is one of the highest-value early constellations in the game.

5. Rotation Guide

Neuvillette Hypercarry rotation:
Neuvillette E → Furina E Q → Kazuha E (tap) Q → Baizhu E Q → Neuvillette CA (hold) → Q → E → CA (hold) × 2 → Repeat

⚠️ Use Neuvillette's E first to generate Sourcewater Droplets. After supports, hold CA to absorb droplets for instant beam. At C1+, you can use 2 charged attacks without re-setup.

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Building ATK on Neuvillette. His entire kit scales with HP. ATK% sands, ATK% substats, and ATK-buffing weapons (except his signature which has CD) are dead stats on him.

❌ Running C0 without a shielder. Neuvillette's charged attack beam can be interrupted by any hit. At C0, you must run a shielder or you will lose significant DPS from canceled beams.

❌ Not triggering all 3 Hydro-related reactions. Neuvillette's A1 passive gives up to 160% CA DMG bonus when 3 different Hydro reactions occur. Build teams that trigger at least 2-3 different reaction types.

❌ Underestimating HP% goblet. With Furina + Kazuha providing massive DMG% buffs, HP% goblet can outperform Hydro DMG goblet if it has better substats. Don't auto-trash HP% goblets with good CRIT rolls.
