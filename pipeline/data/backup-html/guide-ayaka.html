Kamisato Ayaka is the premiere Cryo main DPS: a devastating burst that deals 20 ticks of Cryo DMG, cryo-infused normal/charged attacks after dash, and the best freeze DPS in the game. 4pc Blizzard Strayer gives her 40% free CRIT Rate against frozen enemies, letting you stack CRIT DMG to extreme levels. This guide covers everything you need to build her optimally in v4.8.

2. Best Artifact Sets

0🎯 Best: 4-Piece Blizzard Strayer

Blizzard Strayer gives 20% CRIT Rate vs Cryo-affected enemies and another 20% vs Frozen (40% total). This means you only need 30-45% CRIT Rate from artifacts to hit 70-85% effective CRIT — letting you invest entirely in CRIT DMG and ATK.

Alternative: None — 4pc Blizzard Strayer is mandatory for freeze Ayaka. The 40% free CRIT Rate is too valuable to give up.

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Premium Freeze
Ayaka + Kokomi + Kazuha + Shenhe. The best Ayaka team. Kokomi provides AoE Hydro for permafreeze. Kazuha groups + buffs Cryo DMG. Shenhe provides massive Cryo flat DMG bonus.
Team 2: Budget Freeze
Ayaka + Mona + Sucrose + Diona. F2P-friendly. Mona buffs DMG via Omen. Diona provides shield + Cryo resonance + battery. Sucrose for grouping and Thrilling Tales buff.
Team 3: Mono Cryo
Ayaka + Shenhe + Kazuha + Layla. For unfreezable enemies (bosses). Full Cryo shred + buffing. Layla provides strong shield and Cryo resonance.

5. Rotation Guide

Premium Freeze rotation:
Shenhe E Q → Kazuha E Q → Kokomi E → Ayaka dash (cryo infusion) → NA1 (apply cryo) → E → Q → NA spam

⚠️ Dash before attacking for cryo infusion + 18% Cryo DMG bonus. NA1 before E for Mistsplitter stacks. Use Q when enemies are frozen so they don't escape the burst. Kokomi's jellyfish provides permafreeze.

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Too much CRIT Rate. Blizzard Strayer gives 40% free CRIT vs frozen enemies. Only build 30-45% CRIT Rate. Anything above 45% is wasted.

❌ Not using dash for cryo infusion. Ayaka's dash applies cryo to her attacks. Without it, she deals physical damage. Always dash before your rotation.

❌ Burst missing enemies. Unfrozen enemies can walk out of her burst. Always freeze first, then Q. Use Kazuha/Sucrose to group enemies before bursting.

❌ Building EM. Ayaka is a freeze DPS — she doesn't melt. EM does nothing for freeze. Stack ATK% and CRIT DMG instead.
