Yelan Build Guide

Sub DPS / Support · Hydro · Bow · S Tier

👥 Best Team
Double Hydro / Hyperbloom (HuTao·Yelan·XQ·Zhongli)
📊 Key Stats
HP 30K+ · ER 180-200% · CRIT 70/140 · Q > E >>> NA

Yelan is a top-tier Hydro sub-DPS: off-field coordinated Hydro attacks that trigger on normal attacks, massive personal DMG that scales with HP, and a ramping DMG buff for the active character. She is basically a 5-star Xingqiu with more damage and slightly less Hydro application. This guide covers everything you need to build her optimally in v4.8.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Aqua Simulacra is BiS — massive CRIT DMG and HP% bonus. Favonius Warbow solves all energy problems. Use Favonius unless you have C1 or enough ER from artifacts.

2. Best Artifact Sets

0🎯 Best: 4-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate

Yelan's burst is her entire kit. Emblem converts ER (which she needs anyway) into burst DMG. With 180-200% ER → 45-50% burst DMG bonus. Matches Yelan perfectly.

Alternative: 2pc HP% + 2pc Hydro DMG (while farming Emblem). 4pc Emblem is the goal for most builds.

Sub-stat Priority

  1. Energy Recharge (to 180-200% — C1 lowers this to ~160%)
  2. CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG (1:2 ratio — she needs both)
  3. HP% (more damage — Yelan scales entirely off HP)
  4. Flat HP (minor boost — less efficient than HP%)

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Double Hydro Hu Tao
Hu Tao + Yelan + Xingqiu + Zhongli. Double Hydro resonance (+25% HP) benefits both Yelan and Hu Tao. Massive single-target DMG.
Team 2: Hyperbloom Driver
Yelan + Nahida + Kuki/Raiden + Flex. Yelan provides Hydro for bloom seeds. Kuki triggers hyperblooms. High floor damage.
Team 3: Mono Hydro
Kokomi + Yelan + Kazuha + Xingqiu. Triple Hydro feeds everyone's energy. Kazuha buffs Hydro DMG. Fun and surprisingly effective.

5. Rotation Guide

Double Hydro Hu Tao rotation:
Zhongli hold E → Yelan E Q → Xingqiu E Q E → Hu Tao E → CA spam (jump/dash cancel) → Q (when HP low)

⚠️ Yelan E then Q to pre-funnel particles. Hu Tao's NA spam triggers Yelan's coordinated burst attacks. Yelan's A4 ramping DMG buff peaks during Hu Tao's field time.

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Building ATK instead of HP. Yelan scales ENTIRELY on HP. ATK does literally nothing for her.

❌ Too little ER. Solo Hydro Yelan needs 200%+ ER. Below 180%, burst won't be up. Use Favonius Warbow or an ER sands.

❌ Leveling NA. Yelan's burst triggers off anyone's NA — she never uses her own normal attacks as a sub-DPS.

❌ Using her as solo Hydro in energy-hungry teams. Yelan needs either C1, Sac Bow, or Fav Bow to battery herself solo. Otherwise pair with Xingqiu.
