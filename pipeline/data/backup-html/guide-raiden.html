2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate

Raiden's kit revolves around her burst and ER. Emblem converts ER investment into burst DMG. With 250% ER → 62.5% burst DMG bonus.

Alternative: 2pc Emblem + 2pc Thundering Fury (while farming). 4pc Emblem is the goal.

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Raiden National
The classic. High single-target + AoE. Consistent rotations. Easy to play. Perfect for Abyss.
Team 2: Raiden Hypercarry
Maximum personal damage. Kazuha VV shred + Sara/Bennett ATK buffs. Massive burst numbers. Needs C6 Sara.
Team 3: Raiden Hyperbloom
Full EM build (Triple EM, Dragon's Bane). Triggers hyperblooms from off-field. Highest floor-damage team in the game.

5. Rotation Guide

Raiden National rotation:
Raiden E → Xingqiu E Q E → Bennett Q E → Xiangling Q E → Raiden Q → N3C × 4 → Repeat

⚠️ Raiden's E first for coordinated attack. Bennett before Xiangling so she snapshots the ATK buff.

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Too little ER. Raiden needs 250%+. Below 220%, burst won't be up on cooldown.

❌ ATK% sands without enough ER. ER sands is almost always better.

❌ Not leveling skill. Her E buffs team burst DMG. Level it to at least 8.

❌ Wasting burst on low HP enemies. Initial slash does massive DMG — aim at biggest threat.
