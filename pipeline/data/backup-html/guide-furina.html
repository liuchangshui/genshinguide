Furina Build Guide

💧 Hydro · 🗡️ Sword · S Tier · Sub DPS / Buffer / Healer

The Regina of All Waters. Provides massive team-wide DMG buffs through HP manipulation, summons three Salon Members for off-field DPS, and can switch to healing mode on demand.

Furina is one of Genshin's most powerful and flexible supports: team-wide buffer, off-field sub-DPS, and emergency healer. Her kit revolves around HP manipulation — she drains the party's HP to fuel her summons, then converts every point of HP change into a team-wide DMG bonus. She scales entirely with HP, not ATK. This guide covers everything you need to build her optimally.

2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4-Piece Golden Troupe

Furina's damage comes entirely from her Elemental Skill (Salon Members). Golden Troupe grants +70% skill DMG while off-field — which is exactly where Furina spends her time. This is by far her strongest set.

Alternative: 4pc Tenacity of the Millelith (full support build — provides team ATK buff, lower personal damage). 2pc Golden Troupe + 2pc HP% (Tenacity/Vourukasha) while farming.

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Neuvillette Hypercarry
Neuvillette · Furina · Kazuha · Xilonen. Furina's buff and Hydro Resonance skyrocket Neuvillette's damage. The premier Fontaine team. Consistent, high AoE damage with massive self-sustain.
Team 2: Noelle Furina Core
Noelle · Furina · Gorou/Yun Jin · Flex. Noelle's team-wide healing perfectly offsets Furina's HP drain while driving on-field. Surprisingly strong. Budget-friendly.
Team 3: Hu Tao Double Hydro
Hu Tao · Furina · Yelan · Jean/Healer. Furina keeps Hu Tao below 50% HP naturally while providing a massive DMG% buff. Hydro Resonance + Yelan for more HP scaling.

5. Rotation Guide

Standard Furina rotation (Ousia mode):
Furina E Q → Team Healer (E/Q) → Support Buffs → Main DPS → Refresh Furina E → Repeat

⚠️ Always start in Ousia (dark/damage) mode. Use E first so Salon Members start draining HP and building Fanfare stacks immediately. Burst amplifies the DMG bonus from those stacks. Always bring a team-wide healer — Furina's HP drain will kill your team otherwise. Refresh E before it expires (30s duration).

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Building ATK on Furina. Her entire kit scales with HP. ATK% sands, ATK% weapons, and ATK% substats are wasted stats.

❌ No team-wide healer. Furina drains the entire party's HP. A single-target healer won't keep up. Bring Jean, Baizhu, Xianyun, Charlotte, or a healer on-field DPS.

❌ Leveling Normal Attack talent. Furina's NA has zero scaling relevance. Save your resources for E and Q.

❌ Wrong Energy Recharge. You need 160-180% ER as solo Hydro, or 130-150% with double Hydro. Without enough ER, you can't burst every rotation and your team loses the massive DMG buff.
