Hu Tao Build Guide

Hu Tao is a top-tier Pyro polearm main DPS. Her E sacrifices HP to infuse her attacks with Pyro and massively boost her ATK based on max HP. She deals devastating charged attack DMG when below 50% HP. She requires animation canceling to reach her ceiling — C1 eliminates stamina concerns. This guide covers everything you need to build her optimally in v4.8.

2. Best Artifact Sets

0🎯 Best: 4-Piece Crimson Witch of Flames

Crimson Witch gives Pyro DMG bonus and increases Vaporize/Melt DMG by 15%. With Hu Tao's vape-focused playstyle, this is the most consistent set. 4pc Shimenawa offers higher CA damage at the cost of energy management.

Alternative: 4pc Shimenawa's Reminiscence (higher CA damage, but drains energy — managing burst uptime is harder). 4pc Crimson Witch is the goal for vape consistency.

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Double Hydro
Hu Tao + Yelan + Xingqiu + Zhongli. The best Hu Tao team. Double Hydro resonance (+25% HP) benefits Hu Tao. Massive vape uptime. Zhongli prevents low-HP deaths.
Team 2: VV Vape
Hu Tao + Xingqiu + Kazuha + Bennett. Kazuha swirls Pyro for VV shred + DMG bonus. Bennett's healing is anti-synergy (Hu Tao wants <50% HP) but ATK buff is massive.
Team 3: Funerational
Hu Tao + Yelan + Xingqiu + Xiangling. All gas, no brakes. Triple Pyro/Hydro offense. No shielder — high risk, very high reward. Needs good dodging.

5. Rotation Guide

Double Hydro rotation:
Zhongli hold E → Xingqiu E Q E → Yelan E Q → Hu Tao E → CA spam (C1: dash cancel, C0: jump cancel) → Q (when low HP or iframe)

⚠️ C1: Dash cancel after each charged attack for maximum DPS. C0: Jump cancel to conserve stamina. Use Q at the end of E duration when HP is lowest (33% DMG bonus + low HP passive).

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Building too much ATK. Hu Tao converts HP to ATK with her E. ATK% substats are nearly wasted — focus on HP% and EM instead.

❌ Too little EM. Without EM, vape DMG is significantly lower. Aim for 100-200 EM from subs or weapon. Dragon's Bane solves this automatically.

❌ Not jump/dash canceling at C0. Without canceling, Hu Tao's CA sends her flying and wastes time. Jump cancel (C0) or dash cancel (C1) between each CA.

❌ Healing Hu Tao above 50% HP. Her A4 passive gives 33% Pyro DMG bonus when below 50% HP. Avoid healers — use shields instead (Zhongli).
