Kuki Shinobu Build Guide

The capable and reliable deputy leader of the "Arataki Gang." Please note: capable and reliable are not appellations for the "Arataki Gang," but for their deputy leader in specific.

Kuki Shinobu is a 4-star Electro Sword healer who became one of the most valuable 4-stars in the game with the introduction of Dendro. Her Elemental Skill sacrifices HP to create a ring that heals and applies Electro in an AoE every 1.5 seconds — making her the best off-field Hyperbloom trigger in the game. She consolidates healing and Hyperbloom trigger into one slot, freeing your team for more damage. Level 90 is mandatory for Kuki because Hyperbloom damage scales primarily with character level and EM.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Freedom-Sworn provides the most EM. Iron Sting R5 is the best F2P craftable. Xiphos' Moonlight converts EM into ER for the team. Any EM sword works — Kuki's damage comes from Hyperbloom, not her personal stats.

2 Xiphos' Moonlight ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 510 Elemental Mastery 165 Excellent. Converts EM to ER for Kuki and the team. Solves energy problems for the whole party.

2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4pc Flower of Paradise Lost

Best for Hyperbloom damage. 80 EM + 40% Bloom/Hyperbloom/Burgeon DMG. About 5-8% more Hyperbloom damage than Gilded Dreams.

3. Best Team Compositions

Hyperbloom
👥 Nahida + Xingqiu/Yelan + Kuki + Flex
The team that made Kuki meta. Nahida provides EM buff + Deepwood shred. Xingqiu/Yelan provides Hydro for seeds. Kuki triggers 30K+ Hyperblooms from off-field.
Quickbloom
👥 Alhaitham + Kuki + Yelan/Xingqiu + Nahida
Alhaitham drives on-field while Kuki triggers Hyperblooms from off-field. Alhaitham also triggers Spread. Dual DPS, extremely high team damage.
Aggravate Support
👥 Keqing/Yae + Fischl + Kuki + Kazuha/Sucrose
Kuki provides healing + Electro resonance for energy. Less damage than Hyperbloom but enables non-Dendro teams.

5. Rotation Guide

Hyperbloom rotation:
Nahida E+Q (mark + EM buff) → Xingqiu E+Q (rain swords) → Kuki E (ring, apply Electro) → Flex actions → Kuki E refresh → Repeat

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Not leveling Kuki to 90 — Hyperbloom damage scales primarily with character level. Level 80→90 is a ~34% damage increase. Non-negotiable.

❌ Building HP over EM — Kuki's healing is sufficient even with full EM build. You don't need to sacrifice EM for HP.

❌ Using Electro DMG goblet — Hyperbloom damage only scales with EM and level. Electro DMG bonus does nothing.

❌ Not using C2 if available — C2 extends the ring from 12s to 15s, giving 100% uptime. Huge quality of life improvement.
