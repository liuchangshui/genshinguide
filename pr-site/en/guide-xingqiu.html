Sword Hydro S Support

Xingqiu is a 4-star Hydro Sword sub-DPS who provides the best off-field Hydro application in the game. His Elemental Burst (Raincutter) summons rain swords that attack with your active character, applying Hydro on every hit. Combined with damage reduction, minor healing, and interruption resistance from his Elemental Skill, Xingqiu is the backbone of virtually every Vaporize, Freeze, and Hyperbloom team. Despite being a 4-star, he's considered one of the most valuable characters in Genshin Impact — many players consider him a 'hidden 6-star.'

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Sacrificial Sword R5 is his signature 4-star — the skill reset allows double E for massive energy. Favonius Sword is the F2P alternative for team energy. If using in a team with Raiden, ER requirements drop significantly.

5. Rotation Guide

National rotation:
Raiden E (Electro application) → Xingqiu E+Q (rain swords) → Bennett E+Q (ATK buff) → Xiangling E+Q (snapshot buffs) → Raiden Q (battery + DPS) → Repeat

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Not building enough ER — Xingqiu's burst costs 80 energy. Without enough ER, you can't burst every rotation and lose all your damage and Hydro application.

❌ Using ATK sands without sufficient ER — Xingqiu without burst = useless. ER first, damage second.

❌ Not using Sacrificial Sword if available — the skill reset doubles his energy generation and damage. Even R1 Sacrificial is worth it.

❌ Leveling Normal Attack — Xingqiu's rain swords scale from his Q talent. NA talent only affects the 2 seconds he's on field.
