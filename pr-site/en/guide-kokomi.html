Sangonomiya Kokomi Build Guide

Catalyst Hydro S Support

The Divine Priestess of Watatsumi Island. All of the island's affairs are at this young lady's fingertips.

Sangonomiya Kokomi is a 5-star Hydro Catalyst healer who combines sustained off-field Hydro application with massive team-wide healing. Her Elemental Skill (Kurage's Oath) provides AoE Hydro application and healing every 2 seconds, while her Burst refreshes the skill duration and enhances her normal attacks. Kokomi is the premier comfort pick for Freeze and Bloom teams — she consolidates the healer and Hydro applicator roles into one slot, freeing your team for more damage. While often underestimated, she's one of the most versatile and valuable supports in the game.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Everlasting Moonglow provides HP and Healing Bonus, but Prototype Amber R5 is nearly as good and free. Thrilling Tales of Dragon Slayers R5 is a strong support option that buffs your main DPS.

2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4pc Ocean-Hued Clam

Best for healer Kokomi. Converts healing into physical AoE damage (up to 27K per bubble). Kokomi's consistent healing maxes the bubble easily.

3. Best Team Compositions

Ayaka Freeze
👥 Ayaka + Kokomi + Kazuha + Shenhe
Premium freeze team. Kokomi provides 100% uptime AoE Hydro with her jellyfish. Consolidates healer + Hydro slot. Kazuha groups + buffs Cryo DMG.
Nilou Bloom
👥 Nilou + Kokomi + Nahida + Flex Dendro
Kokomi is the best driver for Nilou Bloom. Her healing offsets self-damage from blooms. Full EM build for maximum bloom damage.
Taser Driver
👥 Kokomi + Fischl + Beidou + Kazuha/Sucrose
On-field Kokomi drives Electro-Charged. Clam set provides additional AoE damage. Very comfortable and safe team.

5. Rotation Guide

Ayaka Freeze rotation:
Shenhe E+Q (Cryo buff) → Kazuha E+Q (Cryo swirl + group) → Kokomi E (jellyfish, Hydro apply) → Ayaka dash + E + Q → Ayaka NA/CA spam → Refresh Kokomi E with Q before it expires

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Building ATK/Crit on healer Kokomi — her passive gives -100% CR. She cannot crit. HP and Healing Bonus are the only stats that matter.

❌ Not refreshing jellyfish — Kokomi's E has 12s duration, 20s cooldown. Use Q to refresh before it expires for 100% uptime.

❌ Using HP goblet over Hydro DMG in on-field teams — during Burst, Kokomi's normal attacks deal significant damage with Hydro goblet.

❌ Ignoring ER — without Prototype Amber or sufficient ER, Kokomi cannot Burst to refresh her jellyfish.
