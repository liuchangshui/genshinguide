Alhaitham is a 5-star Dendro Sword DPS who uses Chisel-Light Mirrors to infuse his attacks with Dendro and deal massive Spread damage. As the Acting Grand Sage of the Akademiya, he's one of the strongest on-field DPS characters in the game, rivaling even Hyperbloom teams with his raw Spread damage. His mirror management creates a high skill ceiling — optimal rotations produce 3-mirror rain attacks that deal devastating AoE Dendro damage. For players willing to learn his mechanics, Alhaitham rewards with top-tier single-target and AoE performance.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Light of Foliar Incision is his signature, scaling with EM. Primordial Jade Cutter and Mistsplitter are strong alternatives. For F2P, Iron Sting R5 provides EM and DMG bonus — excellent value. Harbinger of Dawn R5 is a surprising budget option if you can maintain >90% HP.

3. Best Team Compositions

Spread Hypercarry
👥 Alhaitham + Nahida + Yae Miko/Fischl + Zhongli
Premium Spread team. Nahida provides EM buff + Deepwood shred. Yae/Fischl applies Electro for Quicken. Zhongli shields + further RES shred. Alhaitham triggers Spread on every mirror attack.
Quickbloom
👥 Alhaitham + Nahida + Yelan/Xingqiu + Kuki Shinobu
Hybrid team. Yelan provides Hydro for occasional Hyperblooms. Kuki triggers both Hyperbloom and maintains Quicken aura. Higher floor than pure Spread.
Budget Spread
👥 Alhaitham + Dendro MC + Fischl + Beidou/Kuki
F2P-friendly. Dendro MC holds Deepwood. Fischl + Beidou provide off-field Electro. No 5-star supports needed — still very strong.

5. Rotation Guide

Spread Hypercarry rotation:
Nahida E+Q (EM buff + mark) → Zhongli hold E (shield) → Yae/Fischl E (Electro application) → Alhaitham Q → wait for 2 mirrors → E → 3-mirror NA spam (2 rain attacks) → Charged ATK → 3-mirror NA spam (2 more rain attacks) → Switch out

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Not managing mirrors — losing 3-mirror state drops DPS by ~30%. Learn the Q→wait→E→CA combo.

❌ Using Deepwood on Alhaitham — Deepwood should be on the support (Nahida/DMC). Alhaitham wants Gilded Dreams for personal damage.

❌ Building ATK over EM — Alhaitham's A4 passive converts EM to DMG%. EM is more valuable than ATK for Spread teams.

❌ Bursting without mirrors — always use Q with 0 mirrors for maximum projection attacks, then rebuild with E+CA.
