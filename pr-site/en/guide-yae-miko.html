Yae Miko Build Guide

Catalyst Electro S Support

Lady Guuji of the Grand Narukami Shrine. Also serves as the editor-in-chief of Yae Publishing House. Unimaginable intelligence and cunning are hidden under her beautiful appearance.

Yae Miko is a 5-star Electro Catalyst sub-DPS who deploys Sesshou Sakura turrets that deal continuous off-field Electro damage. As the Guuji of the Grand Narukami Shrine, she excels in Quicken (Aggravate) teams where her turrets consistently trigger the reaction. Her Elemental Burst delivers a massive nuke by consuming all active turrets. While her playstyle requires frequent field time to refresh turrets, Yae Miko rewards with some of the highest off-field Electro DPS in the game, especially when paired with Fischl as a battery and Dendro supports for Aggravate.

3. Best Team Compositions

Aggravate
👥 Yae Miko + Nahida + Fischl + Kazuha
Premium Aggravate. Nahida provides EM buff + Deepwood shred. Fischl batteries Yae and triggers more Aggravates. Kazuha groups + Electro DMG buff. Consistent high DPS.
Raiden Hypercarry
👥 Raiden + Yae + Kazuha + Bennett
Double Electro nuke team. Raiden and Yae alternate bursts. Kazuha buffs Electro. Bennett provides ATK. Massive burst damage window.

5. Rotation Guide

Aggravate rotation:
Nahida E+Q (EM buff) → Kazuha E+Q (Electro swirl + DMG buff) → Fischl E/Q (Oz summon) → Yae E×3 (place turrets) → Yae Q (detonate turrets) → Yae E×3 (replace turrets) → Kazuha E (refresh VV) → Repeat

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Bursting without 3 turrets — each turret consumed adds an extra lightning strike. Without 3, you lose massive damage.

❌ Not refreshing turrets after burst — Q destroys all turrets. Always re-place E×3 immediately after.

❌ Building full EM like Hyperbloom trigger — Yae's Aggravate damage scales with ATK and CRIT as well. Pure EM is only for Hyperbloom teams.

❌ Using Yae as solo Electro — without Fischl battery, Yae needs 150%+ ER and loses significant damage potential.
