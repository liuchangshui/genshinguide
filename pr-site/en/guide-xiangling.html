2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate

4-Piece Emblem of Severed Fate is the optimal set for Xiangling.

Main Stats Priority

SlotMain StatNotes
🕐 Sands ER% / ATK% ER% until 180-200%. Then ATK%.
🍷 Goblet Pyro DMG / ATK% Pyro DMG almost always better.
👑 Circlet CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG Aim for 60%+ CR. XL crits a lot.

Sub-stat Priority

  1. 1. Energy Recharge — 180-200% minimum. Non-negotiable for burst uptime.
  2. 2. CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG — 1:2 ratio. Aim for 60/120.
  3. 3. ATK% — more damage after ER requirements met.
  4. 4. Elemental Mastery — only in reaction teams (Vape/Melt).

6. Common Mistakes

❌ ❌ Not enough ER. Xiangling needs 180%+ to burst every rotation. Below 160% = rotation fail.

❌ ❌ Burst before Bennett Q. XL's burst snapshots ATK buff — always Bennett Q first.
