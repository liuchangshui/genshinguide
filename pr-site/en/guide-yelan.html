Yelan is a top-tier Hydro sub-DPS: off-field coordinated Hydro attacks that trigger on normal attacks, massive personal DMG that scales with HP, and a ramping DMG buff for the active character. She is basically a 5-star Xingqiu with more damage and slightly less Hydro application. This guide covers everything you need to build her optimally in v4.8.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Aqua Simulacra is BiS — massive CRIT DMG and HP% bonus. Favonius Warbow solves all energy problems. Use Favonius unless you have C1 or enough ER from artifacts.

Recommended rotation:
Zhongli hold E → Yelan E Q → Xingqiu E Q E → Hu Tao E → CA spam (jump/dash cancel) → Q (when HP low)

6. Common Mistakes

❌ ❌ Building ATK instead of HP. Yelan scales ENTIRELY on HP. ATK does literally nothing for her.

❌ ❌ Too little ER. Solo Hydro Yelan needs 200%+ ER. Below 180%, burst won't be up. Use Favonius Warbow or an ER sands.

❌ ❌ Leveling NA. Yelan's burst triggers off anyone's NA — she never uses her own normal attacks as a sub-DPS.

❌ ❌ Using her as solo Hydro in energy-hungry teams. Yelan needs either C1, Sac Bow, or Fav Bow to battery herself solo. Otherwise pair with Xingqiu.
