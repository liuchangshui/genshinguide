Faruzan is a 4-star Anemo Bow support who provides the single strongest Anemo DMG buff in the game. Her Elemental Burst applies Anemo RES shred (30% at Lv10) and grants Anemo DMG bonus to the team. Her C6 is one of the most transformative constellations in the game — it generates continuous energy particles, provides 40% Anemo CRIT DMG, and applies her buffs automatically. Faruzan is the dedicated support for Xiao, Wanderer, and any future Anemo hypercarry. Pre-C6 she needs extreme ER investment (250-300%); post-C6 her ER needs drop dramatically.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Favonius Warbow is her best — ER substat + team energy particles. Sacrificial Bow provides ER + skill reset for more energy. Elegy for the End (5-star) provides team EM + ATK buffs. Pre-C6, ER is the ONLY stat that matters.

3. Best Team Compositions

Xiao FFXX
👥 Xiao + Faruzan C6 + Furina + Xianyun
Xiao's best team. Faruzan C6 provides 30% Anemo RES shred + 40% Anemo CRIT DMG + continuous energy. Furina amplifies all damage. Xianyun enables higher plunges.
Wanderer Hypercarry
👥 Wanderer + Faruzan + Bennett + Zhongli
Wanderer's best team. Faruzan buffs Anemo DMG. Bennett provides ATK. Zhongli shields. Consistent high DPS.

5. Rotation Guide

Xiao FFXX rotation:
Faruzan Q (Anemo shred + buff) → Furina E+Q (Fanfare + Hydro) → Xianyun E+Q (heal + plunge buff) → Xiao EE → Q → Plunge spam → Faruzan E (energy funnel to Xiao) → Repeat

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Not building enough ER pre-C6 — Faruzan's 80-cost burst with poor particle generation means she needs 250-300% ER. Without it, you burst once every 3 rotations. This is the #1 mistake.

❌ Leveling NA talent — Faruzan deals zero damage with normal attacks. Her value is entirely in Q and E buffs.

❌ Using ATK weapon pre-C6 — pre-C6, Faruzan cannot burst without ER weapon. ATK weapons are only viable post-C6.

❌ Not funneling particles to Xiao — pre-C6, use Faruzan E then immediately swap to Xiao to catch the Anemo particles. This is essential for Xiao's energy.
