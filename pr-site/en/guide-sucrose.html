Sucrose Build Guide

Catalyst Anemo S Support

An alchemist filled with curiosity about all things. She researches bio-alchemy.

⚡ TL;DR — Quick Answer (30s read)

Sacrificial Fragments R5 (BiS) / Thrilling Tales R5 (support)
🎯 Best Artifact
4pc Viridescent Venerer (EM/EM/EM)
👥 Best Team
Taser (Sucrose·Beidou·Fischl·Xingqiu)
📊 Key Stats
EM 800-900 · ER 150-170% · Level 90 · Level 90

Sucrose is a 4-star Anemo Catalyst support who provides Elemental Mastery sharing, enemy grouping, and Viridescent Venerer RES shred. Her A4 passive shares 20% of her EM with the team, and her C6 provides 20% Elemental DMG bonus. She's the F2P-friendly alternative to Kazuha, and in reaction-heavy teams (Taser, Vaporize, Aggravate), her EM sharing can actually outperform him. Sucrose also serves as the premier on-field driver for Taser teams, swirling Electro-Charged with every normal attack.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Sacrificial Fragments R5 is her best — EM substat + skill reset for double E = double particles + double grouping. Thrilling Tales R5 for pure support: 48% ATK buff for the next character.

2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4pc Viridescent Venerer

Mandatory for all Anemo supports. 60% Swirl DMG + 40% Elemental RES shred. Triples Sucrose's team contribution.

Main Stats Priority

SlotMain StatNotes
🕐 Sands EM Always EM. More EM = more team EM share + more swirl damage.
🍷 Goblet EM Always EM. Anemo DMG goblet adds little — Sucrose's damage is from swirls, not Anemo.
👑 Circlet EM Always EM. Triple EM is the only build for support Sucrose.

Sub-stat Priority

  1. 1. Elemental Mastery — as high as possible, target 800+
  2. 2. Energy Recharge — 150-170% for burst uptime
  3. 3. Flat ATK / ATK% — minor, only for personal Anemo damage
  4. 4. CRIT — irrelevant for full EM build

3. Best Team Compositions

Taser Driver
👥 Sucrose + Beidou + Fischl + Xingqiu
Classic Taser. Sucrose drives on-field, swirling Electro-Charged with every normal attack. Her EM share buffs the entire team's reaction damage.
Vaporize Support
👥 Hu Tao/Diluc + Sucrose + Xingqiu + Bennett/Flex
Sucrose provides VV shred + EM share for the Pyro DPS. Her grouping helps AoE Vaporize hits. Thrilling Tales buff further amplifies damage.
Aggravate Support
👥 Keqing/Yae + Fischl + Sucrose + Nahida/Yaoyao
Sucrose provides VV shred + EM share for the Electro DPS. Her grouping helps AoE Aggravate. Kazuha gives DMG%, Sucrose gives EM — pick based on team needs.

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Building ATK/Crit on support Sucrose — her damage comes from swirls, which only scale with EM and level. ATK/Crit do almost nothing.

❌ Not leveling to 90 — Swirl damage scales with character level. Level 80→90 is a ~34% swirl damage increase.

❌ Using the wrong weapon — Sacrificial Fragments gives EM + double skill. Don't use Mappa Mare or other ATK% catalysts.

❌ Not using VV set — without 4pc VV, Sucrose loses 40% RES shred. Even bad VV artifacts are better than perfect non-VV ones.
