Beidou Build Guide

Claymore Electro S Support

Captain of her crew, The Crux. She's quite an unbound and forthright woman.

Beidou is a 4-star Electro Claymore sub-DPS whose Elemental Burst (Stormbreaker) creates a lightning arc that chains between enemies, dealing massive AoE Electro damage. Her Elemental Skill (Tidecaller) is a unique parry/counter that deals devastating damage when timed correctly. Beidou excels against 2+ enemies — her burst chains 5 times per discharge, making her one of the strongest AoE damage dealers in the game. Paired with Fischl as a battery, she forms the core of the legendary 'Taser' team composition.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Serpent Spine (BP) is her best 4-star — CR substat + DMG bonus. Akuoumaru R5 excels with high team energy. For F2P, Prototype Archaic is a solid craftable. Luxurious Sea-Lord (event) is excellent if you have it.

2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4pc Emblem of Severed Fate

Best overall. Converts ER into Burst DMG. Beidou needs 160-180% ER as solo Electro, so Emblem provides ~40% Burst DMG bonus. Her burst is her primary damage source.

3. Best Team Compositions

Taser
👥 Sucrose + Beidou + Fischl + Xingqiu
The classic. Sucrose drives with normal attacks. Beidou + Fischl provide off-field Electro. Xingqiu provides Hydro. Massive AoE Electro-Charged chains.
Aggravate
👥 Keqing/Yae + Beidou + Fischl + Nahida/Yaoyao
Double Electro Aggravate. Beidou provides AoE + damage reduction. Fischl batteries. Nahida provides EM buff. Strong against 2+ enemies.

Overload team. Beidou provides AoE Electro + interrupt resistance. Chevreuse shreds Pyro/Electro RES.

5. Rotation Guide

Taser rotation:
Fischl E (summon Oz) → Beidou E+Q (snapshot buffs) → Xingqiu E+Q (rain swords) → Sucrose NA spam (driver + swirl) → Beidou E (counter) → Repeat

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Running Beidou as solo Electro without ER — her 80-cost burst needs Fischl battery or 160%+ ER. Without it, you burst once every 3 rotations.

❌ Not using C2 — C2 makes Stormbreaker chain to 5 enemies (from 3). This is her biggest power spike. Worth buying from Paimon's Bargains.

❌ Not snapshotting — Beidou's burst snapshots ATK and DMG% buffs. Always cast buffs first, then Q.

❌ Leveling Normal Attack — Beidou's damage comes from E and Q. NA only matters if she's the on-field driver.
