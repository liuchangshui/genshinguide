Nahida, the Dendro Archon, is the single most impactful Dendro character in Genshin Impact. Her Elemental Skill (All Schemes to Know) marks up to 8 enemies and deals coordinated Dendro DMG whenever any marked enemy takes reaction damage. Her Burst provides massive EM buffs to the active character. She enables Hyperbloom, Burgeon, Aggravate, Spread, and Nilou Bloom — essentially every Dendro team wants her. She scales primarily with EM, but unlike other EM users, she also benefits from CRIT and Dendro DMG due to her A4 passive.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: A Thousand Floating Dreams (EM + team buff) is BiS. Sacrificial Fragments (EM 221) is the best F2P. Magic Guide (3★, EM 187) is a shockingly good budget option. Prioritize EM on weapon — her entire kit scales with it.

2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4pc Deepwood Memories

Deepwood Memories reduces enemy Dendro RES by 30%. This is essential — ALL Dendro damage benefits, including Hyperbloom and Burgeon (which deal Dendro DMG). In a team with two Dendro characters, the other can hold Deepwood and Nahida can use Gilded Dreams for more personal damage.

Main Stats Priority

SlotMain StatNotes
🕐 Sands Elemental Mastery EM sands in almost all cases. ATK% only for C6 on-field DPS build (niche).
🍷 Goblet EM / Dendro DMG EM goblet if below 1000 EM. Dendro DMG goblet if already at 1000 EM (A4 caps at 1000).
👑 Circlet EM / CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG EM circlet for maximum reaction damage. CRIT circlet for on-field Nahida with good substats.

Sub-stat Priority

  1. 1. Elemental Mastery — to 1000 (soft cap for A4 passive). Every point below 1000 matters.
  2. 2. CRIT Rate / CRIT DMG — A4 converts EM to CRIT Rate (max 24%) and DMG bonus (max 80%). Past 1000 EM, CRIT becomes more valuable.
  3. 3. Energy Recharge — 130% minimum. Burst is important but not as critical as E.
  4. 4. ATK% — low value. Nahida's damage scales primarily with EM.

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Hyperbloom
👥 Nahida + Xingqiu/Yelan + Kuki Shinobu/Raiden + Flex
The quintessential Dendro team. Nahida marks enemies, Hydro creates cores, Electro triggers Hyperbloom. Kuki heals and triggers with full EM build. Extremely high floor damage with low investment.
Team 2: Aggravate/Spread
👥 Yae Miko/Fischl + Nahida + Kazuha + Zhongli/Baizhu
Quick-swap Quicken team. Nahida provides EM share for bigger aggravates. Kazuha shreds Electro RES. High single-target damage.
Team 3: Nilou Bloom
👥 Nilou + Nahida + Kokomi + Flex Dendro/Hydro
Only Dendro+Hydro allowed. Nilou creates Bountiful Cores that explode instantly. Nahida provides sustained Dendro application to generate cores. Massive AoE damage.

Hyperbloom rotation:

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Building ATK instead of EM. Nahida's damage scales with EM. Her A4 passive converts EM to CRIT Rate and DMG bonus. ATK does very little for her.

❌ Putting Deepwood on the wrong character. In a team with another Dendro (like Alhaitham), Nahida can use Gilded Dreams while the other holds Deepwood. But in solo Dendro teams, Nahida MUST hold Deepwood.

❌ Not marking enemies with E. Nahida's hold E has a large AoE — mark every enemy before starting your rotation. Unmarked enemies take zero coordinated damage.

❌ Using her with too little ER. While her burst cost is low (50), in teams without a second Dendro, 130%+ ER is still needed for consistent uptime.
