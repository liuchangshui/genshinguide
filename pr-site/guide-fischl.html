Fischl Build Guide

Bow Electro S Support

A mysterious girl who calls herself "Prinzessin der Verurteilung" and travels with a night raven named Oz.

ATK 1700+ · CR 65%+ · CD 140%+ · EM 100+ · E > Q > NA

Fischl is a 4-star Electro Bow sub-DPS whose Elemental Skill summons Oz, a raven that deals continuous off-field Electro damage. Her A4 passive triggers an extra Electro attack every time your active character triggers an Electro-related reaction — making her the best Aggravate enabler in the game. She's also an excellent battery, generating massive Electro particles for energy-hungry teammates like Yae Miko and Beidou. Despite being a 1.0 character, Fischl remains top-tier and is present in almost every Electro-based team composition.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Aqua Simulacra provides massive CD and universal DMG bonus. The Stringless R5 is the best 4-star with EM + Skill/Burst DMG. For pure support, Elegy for the End provides team EM and ATK buffs.

3. Best Team Compositions

Aggravate
👥 Keqing/Yae + Fischl + Nahida + Kazuha/Sucrose
The team where Fischl shines brightest. Her A4 triggers on every Aggravate, doubling her damage output. Nahida provides EM. Kazuha/Sucrose buffs Electro DMG.
Taser
👥 Sucrose + Fischl + Beidou + Xingqiu
Classic Taser. Sucrose drives with normal attacks. Fischl + Beidou provide off-field Electro. Xingqiu provides Hydro. Massive AoE Electro-Charged damage.
Overload (Chevreuse)
👥 Arlecchino/Yoimiya + Fischl + Chevreuse + Bennett
Overload team enabled by Chevreuse. Fischl provides off-field Electro. Chevreuse shreds Pyro/Electro RES.

5. Rotation Guide

Aggravate rotation:
Nahida E+Q (EM buff) → Kazuha E+Q (Electro swirl) → Fischl E (summon Oz) → Keqing/Yae combo → Fischl Q (refresh Oz) → Repeat

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Not refreshing Oz — Oz has 10s (12s at C6) duration. Fischl's Q is used purely to refresh Oz, not for damage.

❌ Casting E before buffs — Oz snapshots. Always apply team buffs before summoning Oz for maximum damage.

❌ Building ER on Fischl — she generates so many particles that ER is wasted. 100% base ER is sufficient.

❌ Using ATK goblet — Electro DMG goblet is always better for sub-DPS Fischl.
