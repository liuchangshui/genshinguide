Albedo Build Guide

Sword Geo S Sub DPS

Albedo is a premium Geo off-field sub-DPS: his E creates a field where enemies take Geo DMG every 2 seconds. His damage scales entirely off DEF, making him easy to build with "unwanted" artifacts. He fits perfectly into Mono Geo teams and any team that wants a flex slot with consistent off-field damage and EM buffing via his Q. This guide covers everything you need to build him optimally in v4.8.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Cinnabar Spindle R5 (event exclusive) is BiS with massive DEF% and E DMG bonus. Harbinger of Dawn R5 (3★) is shockingly competitive for F2P — just keep HP above 90%.

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Mono Geo
👥 Itto + Albedo
Itto + Albedo + Gorou + Zhongli. The premium Geo team. Gorou buffs all Geo, Albedo provides massive off-field DPS, Zhongli shreds RES. Itto is the hypercarry.
Team 2: Double Geo Flex
👥 Albedo + Zhongli
Albedo + Zhongli + any DPS duo. Geo resonance + Zhongli shred + Albedo off-field DMG. Fits Xiao, Hu Tao, Yoimiya — any on-field DPS.
Team 3: Navia Double Geo
👥 Navia + Albedo
Navia + Albedo + Bennett + Xiangling. Albedo generates crystallize shards for Navia. Bennett buffs both. Strong synergy with Navia's Geo infusion.

5. Rotation Guide

Recommended rotation:
Zhongli hold E → Gorou E Q → Albedo E (inside Gorou field) Q → Itto E → Itto Q → CA spam → Albedo refresh E

6. Common Mistakes

❌ ❌ Building ATK instead of DEF. Albedo's E damage scales entirely with DEF. ATK% and flat ATK do almost nothing for him.

❌ ❌ Not placing E inside buff fields. Albedo's E snapshots DEF on cast. Always cast it inside Gorou's buff field or before Bennett Q expires.

❌ ❌ Leveling NA. Albedo is never on-field to use normal attacks. NA level has zero impact on his performance.

❌ ❌ Using 4pc Husk without pre-stacking. Husk needs 4 Geo DMG hits for full stacks. Albedo should be first in rotation so he stacks passively. If placed last, his E snapshots 0 stacks.
