Kamisato Ayaka is the premiere Cryo main DPS: a devastating burst that deals 20 ticks of Cryo DMG, cryo-infused normal/charged attacks after dash, and the best freeze DPS in the game. 4pc Blizzard Strayer gives her 40% free CRIT Rate against frozen enemies, letting you stack CRIT DMG to extreme levels. This guide covers everything you need to build her optimally in v5.6.

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Premium Freeze
👥 Ayaka + Kokomi
Ayaka + Kokomi + Kazuha + Shenhe. The best Ayaka team. Kokomi provides AoE Hydro for permafreeze. Kazuha groups + buffs Cryo DMG. Shenhe provides massive Cryo flat DMG bonus.
Team 2: Budget Freeze
👥 Ayaka + Mona
Ayaka + Mona + Sucrose + Diona. F2P-friendly. Mona buffs DMG via Omen. Diona provides shield + Cryo resonance + battery. Sucrose for grouping and Thrilling Tales buff.
Team 3: Mono Cryo
👥 Ayaka + Shenhe
Ayaka + Shenhe + Kazuha + Layla. For unfreezable enemies (bosses). Full Cryo shred + buffing. Layla provides strong shield and Cryo resonance.

6. Common Mistakes

❌ ❌ Too much CRIT Rate. Blizzard Strayer gives 40% free CRIT vs frozen enemies. Only build 30-45% CRIT Rate. Anything above 45% is wasted.

❌ ❌ Not using dash for cryo infusion. Ayaka's dash applies cryo to her attacks. Without it, she deals physical damage. Always dash before your rotation.

❌ ❌ Burst missing enemies. Unfrozen enemies can walk out of her burst. Always freeze first, then Q. Use Kazuha/Sucrose to group enemies before bursting.

❌ ❌ Building EM. Ayaka is a freeze DPS — she doesn't melt. EM does nothing for freeze. Stack ATK% and CRIT DMG instead.
