Xiao Build Guide

Polearm Anemo S Support

A yaksha adeptus that defends Liyue. Also heralded as the "Conqueror of Demons" and "Vigilant Yaksha."

ATK 2000+ · CR 75%+ · CD 180%+ · ER 120-130%

Xiao is a 5-star Anemo Polearm Hypercarry DPS who deals massive AoE damage through plunging attacks during his Elemental Burst (Bane of All Evil). As one of the original Liyue Big Three, Xiao remains a top-tier DPS with his unique high-mobility playstyle and high damage ceiling. While his HP-drain mechanic and ER requirements demand careful team building, his raw plunge damage and wide AoE make him one of the strongest AoE carries in the game, especially when paired with his dedicated supports Faruzan, Furina, and Xianyun.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Primordial Jade Winged-Spear is his signature and BiS, stacking ATK during combat. Staff of Homa is a close second. For F2P, Blackcliff Pole R5 is the best accessible option.

2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4pc Vermillion Hereafter

Xiao's signature artifact set. After using Burst, gains 8% ATK per HP loss (max 4 stacks = 32% ATK). Perfect synergy with Xiao's HP drain mechanic. However, the domain is resin-inefficient.

3. Best Team Compositions

Premium Plunge (FFXX)
👥 Xiao + Faruzan C6 + Furina + Xianyun
Xiao's best team. Faruzan C6 provides massive Anemo RES shred and DMG bonus. Furina amplifies all damage via Fanfare. Xianyun enables higher plunge damage and team-wide healing.
Classic Hypercarry
👥 Xiao + Faruzan + Bennett + Zhongli
Reliable core. Faruzan buffs Anemo DMG, Bennett provides massive ATK buff, Zhongli's shield prevents interruption during plunges and shreds enemy RES.
Double Geo
👥 Xiao + Albedo + Zhongli + Jean/Bennett
Alternative without Faruzan C6. Geo resonance provides 15% DMG bonus. Albedo provides off-field damage. Jean C4 shreds Anemo RES.

5. Rotation Guide

Xiao FFXX rotation:
Faruzan Q (Anemo buff) -> Furina E+Q (Fanfare + Hydro) -> Xianyun E+Q (heal + plunge buff) -> Xiao EE (skill x2 for energy) -> Q (burst) -> Plunge spam (10-12 high plunges) -> Repeat

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Not pre-funneling energy — use E x2 then immediately Q to catch particles during burst animation.

❌ Using ATK% goblet instead of Anemo DMG% — Xiao's burst converts all damage to Anemo. Anemo goblet is always better.

❌ Ignoring collision plunges — hitting enemy with Xiao's model during plunge adds significant extra damage.

❌ Not managing HP drain — without a healer, Xiao's burst can drain him to critical HP levels.
