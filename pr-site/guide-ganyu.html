Ganyu is a 5-star Cryo Bow DPS and one of the most consistent damage dealers in Genshin Impact. Her Charged Attack Level 2 (Frostflake Arrow) deals massive AoE Cryo damage with zero cooldown, making her top-tier in both Melt and Freeze teams. She excels at ranged combat, overworld sniping, and AoE-heavy Abyss floors. While newer DPS units compete on raw numbers, Ganyu's flexible team options and comfortable playstyle keep her as a top-tier pick for all content.

1. Best Weapons (Ranked)

Bottom line: Amos' Bow is her signature BiS with up to 52% Charged ATK DMG bonus. Aqua Simulacra and Hunter's Path are close alternatives. For F2P, Prototype Crescent R5 rivals 5-star options if you can hit weak points consistently.

2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4pc Wanderer's Troupe

Best for Melt Ganyu. 80 EM bonus + 35% Charged ATK DMG. Ganyu's charged shots are her primary damage source, and the EM buff amplifies Melt reactions significantly.

3. Best Team Compositions

Melt Ganyu (Premium)
👥 Ganyu + Xiangling + Bennett + Zhongli
The classic Melt team. Zhongli's shield lets Ganyu charge shot safely. Bennett buffs ATK, Xiangling applies Pyro. Ganyu's charged shots Melt for 1.5× damage. Best single-target DPS team for Ganyu.
Morgana (Freeze)
👥 Ganyu + Kazuha/Venti + Mona/Kokomi + Diona
AoE Freeze comp. Group enemies with Anemo CC, apply Hydro, freeze everything, then Ganyu's burst deals continuous Cryo damage. Devastating against groupable enemies, struggles against bosses.
Burn-Melt
👥 Ganyu + Nahida + Bennett + Zhongli
Nahida applies consistent Burning (Dendro + Pyro), which maintains Pyro aura for Ganyu to Melt every charged shot. More consistent Pyro application than Xiangling but lower team DPS ceiling.

5. Rotation Guide

Melt Ganyu rotation:
Zhongli hold E (shield) → Bennett Q (ATK buff) → Xiangling E + Q (Pyro application) → Ganyu E (taunt + dodge) → Charged Shot spam (6-7 shots during Pyronado)

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Using ATK% goblet instead of Cryo DMG% — Ganyu's damage is almost entirely Cryo. Cryo DMG goblet is always better.

❌ Over-investing in ER — Ganyu's burst is not her primary damage source in Melt teams. 120% ER is sufficient.

❌ Skipping shield in Melt teams — Ganyu gets interrupted easily during charged shots. Zhongli or Layla shield is essential.

❌ Using Blizzard Strayer in Melt teams — 4pc BS gives no benefit when enemies have Pyro aura. Use Wanderer's Troupe or Shimenawa for Melt.
