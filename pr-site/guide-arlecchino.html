Arlecchino is a top-tier on-field Pyro DPS who scales uniquely with her Bond of Life mechanic. She cannot be healed by allies — instead, her burst is her only source of recovery. This guide covers everything you need to master the Knave in v5.6.

2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4-Piece Fragment of Harmonic Whimsy

Tailor-made for Arlecchino. Grants 18% ATK (2pc). When Bond of Life fluctuates, gain up to 54% DMG bonus (4pc). This set was released alongside her and is mathematically her ceiling.

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Vape Arlecchino
👥 Arlecchino + Yelan + Kazuha + Bennett
👥 Her highest ceiling team. Yelan and/or Xingqiu provide off-field Hydro for consistent Vaporize. Kazuha groups enemies and shreds Pyro RES with VV. Bennett buffs ATK — yes, Bennett is fine even though he heals; his ATK buff far outweighs the anti-synergy. Arlecchino's self-sustain from burst is enough.
Team 2: Mono Pyro
👥 Arlecchino + Xiangling + Kazuha + Bennett
👥 No reactions, just raw Pyro dominance. Xiangling provides off-field damage and Pyro resonance (+25% ATK). Kazuha shreds RES and groups. Bennett buffs both Arlecchino and Xiangling. Consistent, easy to play, less dependent on EM substats.
Team 3: Overload (Chevreuse)
👥 Arlecchino + Fischl + Chevreuse + Bennett
👥 Overload comp enabled by Chevreuse. Fischl provides off-field Electro for Overload triggers. Chevreuse shreds Pyro/Electro RES and provides ATK buff. Bennett rounds out the team. High single-target damage, excellent against bosses. NO HEALERS APART FROM BENNETT. Chevreuse's healing is minimal and optional — her buffs come from her passive, not healing.

6. Common Mistakes

❌ ❌ ❌ Bringing a healer other than Bennett. Arlecchino's A1 passive prevents all ally healing in combat. Kokomi, Baizhu, Xianyun, etc. are dead slots. Bennett is only used for his ATK buff.

❌ ❌ ❌ Using Burst too early. Her Q clears all Bond of Life and heals. If you burst before consuming BoL with NAs, you lose your damage buff and waste uptime.

❌ ❌ ❌ Forgetting to absorb the mark. After using E, you must use a Charged Attack after ~5 seconds to absorb the Bond of Life. Without this, your NAs deal physical damage — almost zero.

❌ ❌ ❌ Not building enough CRIT Rate. Arlecchino has no innate CRIT from ascension (she gets CRIT DMG). Without a CRIT Rate weapon or circlet, you will struggle to reach 70%+ CRIT Rate.

❌ ❌ ❌ Running a shielder instead of offensive support. Zhongli is comfortable but significantly lowers her damage ceiling. Kazuha or Sucrose provide far more team DPS. Learn to dodge.
