Bennett is the single best support character in Genshin Impact. His Elemental Burst provides a massive ATK buff based on his base ATK (character + weapon only — artifacts do NOT count), while healing anyone below 70% HP. He fits into virtually every team as a healer, buffer, and Pyro enabler. At C1, his ATK buff has no HP restriction, making him the undisputed king of supports since version 1.0.

2. Best Artifact Sets

🎯 Best: 4pc Noblesse Oblige

Noblesse Oblige gives +20% ATK to the entire team when Bennett uses his Burst — stacking perfectly with his own ATK buff. There is no better support set for him.

Sub-stat Priority

  1. 1. Energy Recharge — until 200%+. Non-negotiable. Burst must be available every rotation.

  3. 3. Flat HP — minor boost to healing.
  4. 4. CRIT Rate/DMG — only for DPS Bennett builds (not recommended for support).

3. Best Team Compositions

Team 1: Raiden National
👥 Raiden Shogun + Xiangling + Xingqiu + Bennett
The timeless meta team. Bennett buffs Xiangling and Raiden simultaneously. Xiangling snapshots Bennett's ATK buff on her Pyronado. Raiden recharges the entire team. Consistent, high single-target and AoE damage.
Team 2: International
👥 Childe + Xiangling + Kazuha + Bennett
Devastating AoE team. Bennett buffs Xiangling, Kazuha double-swirls Pyro and Hydro. Childe enables Xiangling to vape every hit. High skill ceiling, higher damage ceiling.
Team 3: Ganyu Melt
👥 Ganyu + Xiangling + Zhongli + Bennett
Bennett enables Pyro application for Ganyu's charged shot melts. Shield from Zhongli prevents interruption. Bennett's buff snapshots onto Xiangling's Pyronado for consistent Pyro uptime.
Team 4: Universal Flex Slot
👥 Any Main DPS + Any Sub DPS + Any Support + Bennett
Bennett fits into virtually every team. ATK-scaling DPS characters benefit most. Notable: Wanderer, Yoimiya, Navia, Lyney all love Bennett's buff.

6. Common Mistakes

❌ Building ATK% artifacts. Bennett's buff scales ONLY with base ATK (character level + weapon ATK). ATK% from artifacts does NOTHING for his buff. Only weapon base ATK matters.

❌ Too little ER. Bennett needs 200%+ ER in most teams. Below 180%, his burst won't be ready. Use ER sands if needed — a weaker buff that's always available beats a stronger buff that's never ready.

❌ Leveling Normal Attacks. Bennett almost never uses normal attacks. Save the talent books for his Burst and Skill.

❌ C6 without thinking. Bennett's C6 infuses melee characters with Pyro — this RUINS physical DPS (Eula, Razor) and overrides some elemental infusions (Keqing, Ayaka). Only activate C6 if you're sure your teams want it.

❌ Using Bennett with HP-scaling DPS. Characters like Neuvillette, Yelan, and Furina scale with HP, not ATK. Bennett's ATK buff is wasted on them. Use different supports.
